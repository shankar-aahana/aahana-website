Prakriti · प्रकृति

Your constitution shapes
everything about your skin

Prakriti maps three fundamental forces: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. Each has a unique balance that shapes how your skin behaves, ages, and responds to treatment. Two patients with the same skin tone and the same concerns can need completely different protocols. Prakriti tells us why.

Vata
Air · Movement
Dry, sensitive, expressive. Reacts quickly. Ages early. Needs barrier protection above all.
Pitta
Fire · Transformation
Reactive, photosensitive, pigmentation-prone. Bright when balanced, and flares with heat and stress.
Kapha
Earth · Structure
Thick, resilient, naturally oily. Stays youthful longer, but prone to congestion and laxity.
Our process
01

Understand you

Every plan begins with a thorough intake that includes your health history, skin concerns, lifestyle, and goals. We also offer a Prakriti constitutional assessment as part of this step: a brief Ayurvedic typing that tells us how your skin is built, not just how it looks today.

02

Analyze your skin

Visia clinical imaging anchors every analysis with eight clinical metrics against a database matched to your age and skin tone. Based on your unique goals and concerns, we may also suggest additional testing, such as labs, skin DNA analysis, or skin microbiome testing.

03

Your personalized plan

A treatment plan built from everything we've learned, including your clinical data, your constitution, your skin tone, your goals, and your life. Not a template borrowed from someone else's skin. Every recommendation is specific to you, sequenced correctly, and explained clearly.

The practice

Physician-led.

Aahana means new beginnings and the first rays of sun. It was founded on the belief that exceptional skin care should be physician-led, deeply personalized, and available to everyone.
