Our process
01

Understand you

Every plan begins with a thorough intake: health history, skin concerns, lifestyle, and goals. We also offer an Ayurvedic Prakriti constitutional assessment to tell us how your skin is built, not just how it looks today.

02

Analyze your skin

Visia clinical imaging anchors every analysis with eight clinical metrics matched to your age and skin tone. Where it adds insight, we layer in precision diagnostics like labs, skin DNA, or microbiome testing.

03

Your personalized plan

A treatment plan built from everything we've learned, including your clinical data, your constitution, your skin tone, your goals, and your life. Not a template borrowed from someone else's skin. Specific to you, sequenced correctly, explained clearly.

Dr. Shankar Mundluru
MD · MBA · Medical Aesthetics
Dr. Shankar Mundluru is the founder of Aahana Medical Aesthetics and a physician trained at Duke University, Boston University School of Medicine, and the Wharton School. He brings a rare combination of clinical depth, scientific rigor, and business acumen to the practice of medical aesthetics.
Before founding Aahana, he served as an executive at a luxury skincare brand and continues to advise healthcare innovation startups and venture capital investors, keeping him at the forefront of emerging technology and clinical innovations.
His clinical focus is precision medical aesthetics, drawing on advanced diagnostics, AI, and emerging clinical innovations, with particular expertise in skin of color and all gender identities.
The practice

Physician-led.
Every step.

At most medical aesthetics practices, a physician signs off on a treatment plan and someone else delivers it, sometimes without the doctor ever setting foot in the clinic. At Aahana, Dr. Shankar leads all medical consultations and treatments directly, setting the standard of care for every patient, every time.

Our licensed aesthetician performs Hydrafacial and VI Peel treatments under Dr. Shankar's direct clinical oversight. Every provider at Aahana works within a physician-led framework. That's not the industry standard. It's ours.

Aahana is built for everyone, with deep expertise across all skin tones, gender identities, and backgrounds. We have a particular commitment to patients who have felt overlooked, rushed, or underserved by conventional aesthetics. Every person deserves a plan built specifically for their skin.
