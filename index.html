Prakriti · प्रकृति

Your constitution shapes
everything about your skin

Prakriti identifies three fundamental constitutional forces — Vata, Pitta, and Kapha — and their unique combination in you determines your skin's characteristics, its sensitivities, its aging patterns, and its response to treatment.

At Aahana, Prakriti typing is central to how we practice — not an add-on, not a marketing angle. It's an optional assessment every patient can complete, and it's the layer that makes the difference between a good plan and one that's genuinely built for your skin.

Vata
Air · Space · Movement
Governs movement, circulation, and the nervous system. In the skin: dryness, delicacy, sensitivity. Reacts quickly to environment and stress. Ages early, needs barrier protection above all.
Pitta
Fire · Water · Transformation
Governs metabolism and transformation. In the skin: reactivity, photosensitivity, and pigmentation tendency. Bright and clear when balanced — flares with heat, hormones, and stress.
Kapha
Earth · Water · Structure
Governs structure, stability, and moisture. In the skin: thickness, resilience, natural oiliness. Stays youthful longer but prone to congestion and sluggishness. Ages with laxity.
