Prakriti

प्रकृति · Your natural constitution

In Ayurvedic medicine, Prakriti describes your unique constitutional makeup. the combination of elemental forces that shapes how your skin behaves, ages, and responds to treatment. Understanding it changes everything about how we care for you.

What is Prakriti

The science of
your skin's nature

Prakriti. from the Sanskrit for "nature" or "original form". is the Ayurvedic framework for understanding individual constitution. It holds that every person is born with a unique combination of three fundamental forces, or doshas, that governs their physiology, their tendencies, and their relationship with the world.

In medical aesthetics, Prakriti typing tells us things no scan can fully capture. how your skin responds under stress, how it ages, what it craves, and what inflames it. Two patients can have identical Fitzpatrick types and entirely different treatment needs. Prakriti explains why.

At Aahana, Prakriti assessment is offered to every patient as part of the intake process. It takes minutes, and it informs everything that follows.

Pitta skin
Warm, sensitive, and photosensitive. Prone to redness, rosacea, and pigmentation.
Bright and luminous when balanced. flares with heat, spice, hormonal shifts, or stress.
Requires special attention to sun exposure and laser wavelengths across all skin tones.
Responds well to cooling, anti-inflammatory treatments and careful energy device selection.

कफ

Kapha
कफ · Earth & Water
Stability, Structure, Endurance
Kapha skin
Thick, oily, and resilient. Naturally moisturized with excellent elasticity and slow aging.
Stays youthful longer than other constitutions, but prone to congestion, enlarged pores, and laxity over time.
Benefits from stimulating, exfoliating, and lifting treatments to counteract sluggishness.
Responds well to microneedling, RF treatments, and deep cleansing protocols.
Resilient and naturally moisturised. Stays youthful longer than other types. but needs stimulation and clearing to stay at its best.
The 42 profiles

Your constitution and
your skin tone together

Prakriti alone doesn't complete the picture. Combined with your Fitzpatrick skin type. which determines your melanin concentration, UV response, and laser safety parameters. it creates one of 42 unique skin profiles.

Each profile carries different clinical implications: different device settings, different ingredient sensitivities, different aging trajectories. Understanding which profile you belong to allows Dr. Shankar to make recommendations that are calibrated not just for "your skin type," but for you specifically.

The Fitzpatrick Scale · Understanding skin tone

The Fitzpatrick scale classifies skin by melanin concentration and UV response, and it's a foundational clinical tool. But at Aahana, we don't stop there. We've developed our own skin profile system that layers Fitzpatrick typing with Ayurvedic Prakriti assessment to create something no standard scale offers: a complete picture of your skin's constitution, behavior, and clinical needs.
